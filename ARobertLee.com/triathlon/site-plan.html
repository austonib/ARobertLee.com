Site Plan

Site Name:

Prison to Prison Triathlon (www.arobertlee.com/triathlon)

Site Purpose:

This website is for anyone in the bay area that would like to participate in or get information about the Prison to Prison Triathlon. This site will provide a way to register for the race and show what all the different legs are and what will be involved.

Target Audience Description:

The target audience for this site is people who are into triathlons, marathons, half-marathons, or just races in general. Target audience members also include the support teams for the participants or even sponsors. Anyone interested in the Prison to Prison Triathlon.

Male and Female Personas:

Name: John

Job: Construction Worker

Demographics: 35 years old, married with no children.

Goals: Wants to become a manager at the construction where he works. He likes to exercise and stay in shape. It makes his job easier and helps him not be so tired at the end of the day. He likes to run with his wife and he would like to participate in a marathon some day with her.

Environment: He loves his job and there are always new exciting construction projects in and around the bay area. He and his wife have a lot of time to exercise and go for runs.

Quote: “I love the rush I get when I run!”

Name: Jane

Job: Retired school teacher

Demographics: 50 years old. She started a family young and her children are all grown and moved out now with families of their own. Married for almost 30 years. Her husband doesn’t understand her love of swimming.

Goals: She loves to quilt and stay active especially after retiring from her job as a school teacher. She loves swimming and wants to complete the swimming leg of a triathlon.

Environment: Has lived her whole life in the Bay Area and loves it there. She loves to hike and spend time outdoors. She has always been fond of swimming.

Quote: “I have to keep active or I’ll die of boredom!”

Scenario list:

  • Will this site allow me to register?
  • Will this site show me a map?
  • Will this site give me historical information about the Prisons?

Content list:

  • Will this site allow me to register?
    • Yes. There will be a page that will allow you to type in your information and register for the race.
  • Will this site show me a map?
    • Yes. There will be a map included on the home page to show the route.
  • Will this site give me historical information about the Prisons?
    • There will be some historical information provided as the key locations of the race are discussed. Most historical information about the Prisons will be linked outside of the Triathlon site.

Color Scheme:

This is challenging to me because I don’t really know how to show the look and “feel” of prison…while showing the beauty and wonder of the bay area. I would like to use the darker colors for the navigational bar and have the lighter hues be for links. (the light yellow or the light blue). Links will be one of the lighter blue colors. accents and logos can be some of the yellow. I want to use lighter grey colors as well. I am colorblind so I have to rely a lot of pallet generators and feedback from others.

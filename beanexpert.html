How To Be An Expert

Some of you will want to take your training to the next level and there are many ways to do that.

Organized Activities

You would be surprised at how many organized activities and opportunities there are to practice your survival skills with like-minded people. If you really want to take your skills past the level of camping or hiking, you can look up one of these programs and get involved.

Your Neck Of The Woods

A good way to take your skills to the next level is to become familiar with the parks and other areas around your own location. This is, after all, where you are likely to go on a weekend getaway and use the skills that you have been learning. One resource is Freecampsites.net. Just type in your location and away you go.

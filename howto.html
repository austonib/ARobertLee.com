How To Survive in the Wild

In order to survive in the wild, you are going to need some skills. If you look through some of the following pages, you will be much better equipped to spend a night or two on your own away from the comforts of home. With practice, your confidence will grow and you will be able to get yourself out of some sticky situations if need be! Remeber the Rule of Threes and you'll know what to work on first.
